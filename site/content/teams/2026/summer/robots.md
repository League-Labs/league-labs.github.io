The Summer 2026 Robots team focuses on building, testing, and demonstrating physical robot systems.

Areas of work:

* assembling and improving robot kits
* firmware and control experiments
* drivetrain, sensors, and attachments
* preparing repeatable demos for other students

Suggested outputs:

* a documented reference build
* a simple control program or test routine
* notes for future class activities and demos
