The Robotics subteam handles hardware, low-level control, and integration on the robot platform.

Areas of work:

* Microcontrollers (Micro:bit/Arduino) bring-up and drivers
* Motor control, sensors, and teleoperation
* Mechanical integration and 3D printed mounts
* Interfaces to Vision inputs and Simulation outputs

Deliverables:

* A reference robot build guide and BOM
* Example control sketch/firmware
* A simple protocol for receiving vision detections and sending telemetry
